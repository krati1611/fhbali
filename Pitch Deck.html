The Project

Four villas at Nyang Nyang, on Bali's Bukit.

A boutique hotel by MarMar Studio — perforated terracotta brick screens, eggshell-cream lime stucco and single-slope ironwood-shingle roofs. Each villa is a single bedroom that opens through glass to its own terrace and pool.

The Website

One scroll,
from sunrise to stay.

A single-page, scroll-driven site. It opens on a cinematic day-arc — sunrise to starlight — then unfolds: the place, a 3D build of the villas, the interiors, the four headboards, the grounds, and the project film. One continuous scroll, from first light to “stay.”

FH · Nyang Nyang
03
Signature Moment · The Opening

From sunrise, to us.

The site opens on a horizontal, scroll-driven carousel — six scenes across a single day. Dawn on the cove, coffee for two, the pool at midday, golden hour in the garden, the corridor at dusk, and the two of you under the stars. A sun-track marker arcs across the day as you scroll, each scene carrying its own line.

The Grounds

Through the gate, into the trees.
